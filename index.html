Portus Interlink

Transform your folders into local servers accessible from your network

What is Portus Interlink?

Portus Interlink is an open-source tool that turns any folder on your device into a local web server. This allows you to access the content from any other device connected to your home or work network. Ideal for developers, educators, gamers, or anyone looking for a quick way to share files without relying on the cloud.

Thanks to its intuitive interface, you can get started in seconds without needing advanced technical knowledge.

Key Features

Quick Setup

Download, choose a folder, set the port... and done! Your server is live.

Cross-Platform Access

Connect from mobiles, tablets, smart TVs, or any browser using the local IP.

No Cloud, No Risk

Securely share files on your network without uploading them to external services.

Benefits of Using Portus

Perfect for Education

Share content with your students without needing emails or USB drives.

Professional & Personal

Developers can test websites on the network, and families can easily share media.

Great for Gamers

Turn your mod or map folders into a server accessible for LAN parties.

Download and Install

Portus Interlink is freely available on GitHub. It's open-source software and continuously updated by its community.
